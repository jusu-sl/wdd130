Kenema

Kenema is a traditional city located in the Eastern part of Sierra Leone. Kenema is the distrist Head quater in the Eastern Province which is belief to be the second most populated city after freetown. The majority of its inhabitants are the mendes though there are other ethnic groups but the mendes are in vast majority. Kenema is a beautiful city with lots of social amenities. The major roads in this city are: The Hangha road, combema road, Blama road and Dama road.
